About

{{ settings.title }} helps people find, understand and use information from privacy notices.

Individuals can learn how to exercise their new rights under GDPR, find out what information is collected about them and see who to contact when they want to know more.

Businesses can understand how service providers share data with third parties and how data is protected outside the EEA.

Developers can use our API to build services that use data from privacy notices in new and empowering ways.
